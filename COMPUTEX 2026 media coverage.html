07 Conclusion

Do more of what worked — go deeper

The growth came from earned, on-message coverage — so the next cycle compounds it three ways.

① More depth interviews

  • Interviews grew +7 YoY → 47 and produced our most on-message coverage
  • They concentrate in the B2B-pivot markets — JP, KR, MEA, MY, PH
  • Target +50% next cycle: one deep interview beats ten PR pickups

② Sharper arguments

  • "Local on-prem AI + digital sovereignty" provably lands — codify it
  • Arm spokespeople with audience-specific, number-backed talking points
  • Give APM & surveillance their own storyline, not DSM's shadow

③ Proactive pitches

  • PR pick-ups fell −15 YoY — stop leaning on the wire
  • Build dedicated pitches: PSIRT, VMware migration, cost-of-cloud
  • Convert this year's EU prosumer contacts into a standing track
